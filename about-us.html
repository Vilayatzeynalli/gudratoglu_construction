Our Success Principles

History

"Gudratoglu Industrial Construction Investment Corporation" Open Joint Stock Company, which contributed to the development of the non-oil sector in Azerbaijan and managed to create new standards in the field of construction, was founded in 2005 and has managed to stand out for its many achievements since then.

In 2015, Gudratoglu Corporation proudly celebrated its 10th anniversary. We can proudly say that Gudratoglu Corporation, one of the leading companies in the construction sector in the South Caucasus, has achieved high achievements in the field of construction and sale of residential buildings, construction of other public facilities and infrastructure projects, production and processing of construction materials in a short period of time.





2008

Gudratoglu Corporation completed the first concrete road project (Baku-Russian Federation Border Highway) in Azerbaijan



2009

Fist time won a contract outside Azerbaijan; Established offices in Georgia and Kazakhstan



2010

Undertook construction of a military facility in Afghanistan



2011

Entered market of Uzbekistan and Ukraine; Started new business area of water and sewerage construction; Completed number of projects in Georgia



2012

Entered market of Turkey; Completed large-scale projects with partners in Ukraine



2013

Expanding its scope of activities, the Corporation has begun construction of the 37 km state-owned concrete road Shymkent-Tashkent, financed by the Asian Development Bank, in the Republic of Kazakhstan, far from the borders of the Republic. Experienced specialists were involved in the construction of the project, which was successfully completed ahead of schedule.



2014

Gudratoglu Corporation in a consortium with SICIM S.p.A.(Italy) and Yuksel Inshaat A.Ş. (Turkey) participated in the tender announced for the construction of TANAP and TAP energy projects to deliver natural gas to be produced from Azerbaijan’s Shah Deniz-2 gas field to Europe via Turkey and Gudratoglu Corporation was among the winners according to the results declared by “TANAP Doghalgaz Iletim A.Ş”



2015

Gudratoglu Corporation has successfully completed micro tunnelling project near Olympic Stadium. By virtue of the planned structural changes conducted within the Corporation, 15 subsidiaries (LLC) were established in different production areas. New manufacturing trends on fiber concrete, claddings, plastic doors and windows were applied. Based on state-of-the-art technologies newly reconstructed Steel Structures Plant was put into operation in Khirdalan



2016

In addition to its domestic projects in the field of water and sewerage, Akkord has transferred its experience to the Republic of Turkey and implemented the construction of a sewerage network and collector lines in the city of Mardin with the financial support of the European Union.



2017

The company took an active part in the tender process outside the country. As a result, the water supply project of Chiatura city of the Republic of Georgia was launched and successful cooperation was established with customers.



2018

Corporation Expansion of Ganja-Gazakh-Georgia border road into 4 lanes, Lot3: Kirli-Georgia border section, km 82 + 200-km 130 + 150, 48 km ”and“ Reconstruction of water supply and sewerage system of Gazakh city ”financed by international banks of state importance ”Project has been launched. In addition, the project on construction of water and sewerage systems of Jalilabad city, consisting of 4 lots, was successfully completed and put into operation.



2019

Completed and commissioned the 100 km long 4-lane road in the Republic of Uzbekistan, sponsored by the Islamic Development Bank. Along with this project, the restoration and reconstruction of existing canals and facilities in the Andijan region have begun.



2020

The corporation won the tender on "Implementation of reclamation measures on winter pastures in Sabirabad and Salyan districts" (Sabirabad district, stage II) implemented at the expense of the local budget and launched the project.
