Our Success Principles

Our principles govern our approach to sensitive topics such as safety, health and the environment. At the same time, these principles determine the attitude we show to people who trust us.

With diversified and dynamic management skills, we are service professionals.

Along with traditional contracts, we operate on the basis of programs, collaborations and established joint ventures. Our strategy is always open to change and new ideas, including supporting a collaborative approach in all areas of activity.

Fulfilling our obligations to customers, employees and society in general

Bringing excellence and professionalism to all aspects of our business

Searching for ways of sustainable development by studying all aspects of the field in which we operate

Making sure we conduct our business correctly and make decisions within an ethical framework

History

"Gudratoglu Industrial Construction Investment Corporation" Open Joint Stock Company, which contributed to the development of the non-oil sector in Azerbaijan and managed to create new standards in the field of construction, was founded in 2005 and has managed to stand out for its many achievements since then.

In 2015, Gudratoglu Corporation proudly celebrated its 10th anniversary. We can proudly say that Gudratoglu Corporation, one of the leading companies in the construction sector in the South Caucasus, has achieved high achievements in the field of construction and sale of residential buildings, construction of other public facilities and infrastructure projects, production and processing of construction materials in a short period of time.
